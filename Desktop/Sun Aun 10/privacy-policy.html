Last Updated: August 16, 2023

"The Digital Chief of Staff" ("I", "my", "me") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit my website.

I may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to me when you fill out a contact form or request a consultation. This information may include:

  • Name

  • Phone Number
  • Any other information you choose to provide in the message field

Like many websites, I may also collect information automatically as you navigate the site, such as your IP address and browser type, to help me understand website traffic and improve the user experience.

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect to:

I will not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

Data Security
